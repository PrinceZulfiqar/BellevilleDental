Yesmin Kassem

GP- General Dentist

Dr. Yesmin Kassem is a Swedish board-certified GP Dentist with 6 years of private clinical experience in Sweden. She earned her degree from Medical University of Debrecen-Faculty of Dentistry in 2016. Dr. Yesmin’s major fields of interests include restorative and cosmetic dentistry. She is a certified Invisalign provider and has attended several courses in Prosthodontics. Dr. Yesmin always seeks to provide high quality and pain-free dental treatments. With her calm and welcoming approach, she is able to cope with anxious and nervous patients and establish a trusting environment.

Murat Guzel

GP Dentist-Implantologist

Dr Murat is a Danish GP dentist with 14 years’ experience in all fields of dentistry. He graduated from the Royal Dental College, University of Copenhagen, Denmark in 2008. After 10 years of private practice in his own clinic he decided to move to Dubai with his family. Dr Murat has special interest and experience in the fields of restorative, cosmetic, reconstructive and surgical dentistry. He is a licensed Implantologist in Dubai. His broad experience and skills allow him to tailor comprehensive treatment plans for his patients and to service his patients in every step from minor restorative treatments to more complicated surgical and prosthodontic rehabilitations.

Tareq Y Shabani

Director Of Dentistry Department

As a Cosmetic and Reconstructive Dentist, Dr. Tareq Shabani has more than 15 years’ experience in the industry. Throughout his career, he has established himself by always using the latest techniques in smile transformations and dental reconstructions and adopting the best patient-care practices. Through his passion for dentistry, skills, and artistic vision, Dr. Tareq has transformed thousands of smiles, from the United States to the UAE. In order to stay abreast of the latest cosmetic dentistry advancements, Dr. Tareq continues to invest in advanced & cutting-edge cosmetic and reconstructive dentistry, orthodontics, and dentistry advancements.

Lana Al Habash

Specialist Pediatric Dentistry

Dr. Lana worked as a Pediatric Dentist in France for two years and in Syria for three years. It was there she realized her love and natural ability for treating children. She moved to Dubai in 2012 and continued her practice as a Specialist in Pediatric Dentistry at various acclaimed hospitals before joining American Hospital Dubai. She has also been a member of the teaching staff in the International University for Science and Technology, School of Dental Medicine (Pediatric Dentistry Department).

Maria Perez Morales

Specialist Endodontist

Dr. Maria Perez Morales is a Venezuelan Dentist and Specialist in Endodontist from Central University of Venezuela (UCV), and Master in Dental Research from the International University of Catalunya (UIC) with more than 25 years of experience in the Dental field. She is skilled in new technologies, an expert in the use of the Dental Microscope, and an active researcher in the study of shaping ability and the metallographic characteristics of the Endodontics instruments using micro-CT technology. Actually, she is finalizing the PhD program in Health Sciences at the International University of Catalunya (Barcelona, Spain). Dr. Maria is a Member of the American Association of Endodontics (AAE), Venezuelan Endodontics Society (SVE), and Emirates Dental Association. Dr. Maria can provide all the services related to her specialty Root Canal Therapy (RCT), Dental Traumatology, new therapies in Revascularization, Apexification, and Oral Pathology

Deborah Rello

Specialist Dental Hygienist

Dr. Deborah Rello is one of the most-sought after Implantologists and Cosmetic Dentists in Dubai. Dr. Rello received her Dentistry Degree at the International University of Catalonia, Spain, in 2003, and went on to complete her Postgraduate studies in Periodontics and Oral Surgery in 2006. She worked for over a decade in Barcelona, Spain and 2 years in Brighton, UK before moving to UAE.

Firas Alnabolsi

Specialist Prosthodontist Cosmetic Dentist

Dr Firas Alnabolsi graduated from Damascus University in 2004 and finished his specialty in Paris, France 2010, University hospital of Pitie Salpetiere and went back to Syria where he was a member of the teaching staff at the private University of Kalamoon for 2 years. Practicing in UAE since 2012, Doctor Firas has helped a lot of his patients and many TV and social life celebrities to find their perfect smile using digital face analysis and digital smile design and working on total oral rehabilitation by replacing missing teeth using dental implants or ceramic bridges and reconstructing broken and crooked teeth using full ceramic crowns in order to regain healthy beautiful smile. His motto in his practice is: “Perfection is not a detail but all details make perfection”.
